Grading & Progression at Iron Academy

At Iron Academy, your progress is important to us! Ms. Cherry offers personalized feedback and recognition for your hard work through our unique video submission system. Learn how to advance in your chosen martial art below.

Traditional Shaolin Kung Fu: Mastering Forms

Traditional Shaolin Kung Fu emphasizes deep mastery of forms, techniques, and underlying principles, rather than a formal belt system. Your progression is about developing skill and understanding through consistent practice.

How to Get Assessed:

  1. Practice Thoroughly: Work through the detailed text instructions for the forms and techniques on the Shaolin Kung Fu course page.
  2. Record Your Performance: When you feel ready, record yourself performing the specific form or techniques you wish to have assessed.
    • Video Guidelines: Ensure clear lighting, full body visibility, and stable camera work. Show the form from front and side angles if possible.
  3. Submit Your Video: Upload your video to a cloud service (like Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive) and ensure the link is shareable.
    Email the link to: shaolingrading@ironacademy.com
    Subject Line: "Shaolin Assessment - [Your Name] - [Form/Technique]"
  4. Receive Feedback: Ms. Cherry will review your submission and provide detailed, personalized feedback via email within 7-10 business days. This feedback will highlight strengths and areas for improvement.

Recognition & Achievement:

Upon satisfactory demonstration of mastery in specific forms or techniques, you will receive "Certificate of Shaolin Kung Fu Achievement" from Iron Academy, recognizing your dedication and skill.

Progression is about continuous growth and perfecting your forms. Each form mastered builds upon the last.

WTF Taekwondo: Structured Belt System

Iron Academy proudly follows the official WTF (World Taekwondo Federation) belt system for our Taekwondo curriculum. Progress through colored belts signifies your increasing skill, knowledge of poomsae, and physical ability.

How to Grade for Your Next Belt:

  1. Study Your Curriculum: Thoroughly learn all techniques, poomsae, and theory for your next belt level from the WTF Taekwondo course page.
  2. Record Your Grading Video: Perform the required poomsae, basic techniques, breaking techniques (if applicable), and any theoretical explanations.
    • Video Guidelines: Ensure the entire form is visible, techniques are clear, and your power and focus are evident.
  3. Submit Your Video: Upload your video to a cloud service and ensure the link is shareable.
    Email the link to: tkdgrading@ironacademy.com
    Subject Line: "Taekwondo Grading - [Your Name] - [Current Belt] to [Next Belt]"
  4. Receive Assessment & Belt Promotion: Ms. Cherry will evaluate your performance according to WTF standards and provide detailed feedback via email within 7-10 business days. Upon successful grading, you will be awarded an official Iron Academy WTF Taekwondo Belt Certificate, promoting you to your next rank!

WTF Taekwondo Belt Order:

  • White Belt (10th Gup)
  • Yellow Belt (9th Gup)
  • Orange Belt (8th Gup)
  • Green Belt (7th Gup)
  • Blue Belt (6th Gup)
  • Red Belt (4th Gup)
  • Black Belt (1st Dan) and higher Dan ranks

Advance through the official WTF belt system with clear goals for each rank.

General Grading Notes:

  • Be Patient: Due to the volume of submissions, feedback may take up to 10 business days.
  • Quality Matters: Clear video quality and adherence to guidelines will help Ms. Cherry assess your skills accurately.
  • Continuous Learning: Grading is a snapshot of your progress. Keep practicing and refining your techniques!
  • Safety First: Always train in a safe environment and never attempt techniques beyond your capability.
