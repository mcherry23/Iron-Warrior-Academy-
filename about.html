About Ms. Cherry & Iron Academy

Meet Your Instructor: Ms. Cherry

Hello! My name is Ms. Cherry, and I'm the founder and instructor here at Iron Academy. At 23 years old, I created this website because I saw a need for a structured and organized place for martial arts enthusiasts to practice, learn, and grow, completely free.

I'm not a grandmaster or a decades-long veteran of martial arts, but I am someone with an immense passion for these incredible disciplines. My journey began with a deep fascination for Traditional Shaolin Kung Fu and WTF Taekwondo. Through dedicated self-study and tireless practice, I've immersed myself in these arts.

While I may be self-taught in my approach to learning, I've also sought external validation for my progress. I've been graded by professional martial artists in both disciplines, and while I haven't yet reached a master's rank, these experiences have been invaluable in shaping my understanding and refining my techniques.

My goal with Iron Academy is simple: to share my enthusiasm and the knowledge I've gained to create a supportive environment. Whether you're interested in the ancient forms of Traditional Shaolin Kung Fu, the practical applications of The Warrior Path, or the dynamic kicks of WTF Taekwondo, I believe in the power of consistent practice and community.

Iron Academy is a place for us to learn together, push our limits, and develop discipline, strength, and confidence. I'm excited to guide you on your martial arts journey!

Our Mission at Iron Academy

Iron Academy was founded on the belief that access to quality martial arts education should be universal. Our mission is to provide free, structured, and passionate instruction in Traditional Shaolin Kung Fu, The Warrior Path, and WTF Taekwondo. We aim to:

  • Empower Individuals: Provide tools for physical and mental growth.
  • Foster Discipline: Encourage consistent practice and dedication.
  • Build Community: Create a supportive space for students worldwide.
  • Promote Accessibility: Break down financial barriers to martial arts training.

Under Ms. Cherry's guidance, we strive to make the benefits of martial arts training available to everyone, fostering a global community of dedicated practitioners.
