Our Free Online Courses

Iron Academy offers comprehensive, free online training across three unique martial arts disciplines. Each path is designed to build your skills, discipline, and confidence under the guidance of Ms. Cherry. All lessons are provided as detailed text instructions, organized into clear levels or forms.

Traditional Shaolin Kung Fu

Immerse yourself in the ancient art of Traditional Shaolin Kung Fu. This path emphasizes historical forms, powerful stances, precise techniques, and the rich philosophy that underpins one of the world's most revered martial traditions.

Progression: Traditional Shaolin Kung Fu does not use a belt system. Your journey is measured by mastery of forms, depth of understanding, and continuous self-improvement. Ms. Cherry will guide you through foundational drills to complex empty-hand and potential weapon forms.

Curriculum & Lessons:

  • Foundational Drills: Stances, Footwork, Basic Strikes
  • Small Hong Quan (Xiao Hong Quan): Form Breakdown & Details
  • Da Hong Quan (Da Hong Quan): Form Breakdown & Details
  • Lian Huan Quan (Connecting Form): Instructions & Applications
  • And More...

WTF Taekwondo

Train in the dynamic and powerful art of WTF Taekwondo. This curriculum focuses on explosive kicks, precise hand techniques, and the traditional forms (poomsae) recognized globally. Follow a structured progression through the official WTF belt system.

Progression: Iron Academy follows the WTF Taekwondo belt system for clear, structured advancement. Your progress will be measured through video grading of poomsae, basic techniques, and breaking skills.

WTF Belt Progression & Lessons:

  • White Belt (10th Gup): Fundamentals
  • Yellow Belt (9th Gup): Taegeuk Il Jang
  • Orange Belt (8th Gup): Taegeuk Ee Jang
  • Green Belt (7th Gup): Taegeuk Sam Jang
  • And More through Black Belt...
