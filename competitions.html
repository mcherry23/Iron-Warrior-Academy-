Challenge & Growth

At Iron Academy, our primary focus is on personal growth, skill development, and self-mastery through dedicated practice.

Formal competitions are not currently offered. They are coming soon.

Your Journey, Your Challenge

  • Challenge Yourself: Push your physical and mental limits.
  • Track Your Progress: See how far you've come by reviewing your own practice.
  • Strive for Perfection: Continuously refine your techniques and understanding.
  • Develop Discipline: The greatest competition is often with yourself.

We encourage you to set personal goals, practice consistently, and use the Grading & Progression system to receive feedback from Ms. Cherry and acknowledge your achievements.
